# RK Coaching LMS - Teacher Operations Guide

Welcome! This guide assists teachers and curriculum designers in managing syllabus nodes, linking lecture content, and authoring interactive quizzes.

---

## 🎒 Part 1: Subject Course Setup

As a coordinator, you can manage syllabus lines for your assigned subjects:

1. **Course Creation**:
   - Navigate to the **Teacher/Admin Dashboard**.
   - Select the respective **Class** (e.g., Class 12 Science) and **Subject** (e.g., Physics).
   - Define a course banner, subtitle, price, and whether the course is **Premium** (requires payment) or **Free**.

---

## 📹 Part 2: Uploading Videos & Revision Notes

Lessons require both visual explanations and printable references:

1. **Adding Chapters & Lessons**:
   - Create a chapter (e.g. *Chapter 1: Electrostatics*).
   - Under that chapter, add individual sequential lessons (e.g. *Lesson 1.1: Coulomb's Law*).

2. **Linking Media**:
   - **Video Lectures**: Insert public stream URLs (such as YouTube Embeds, Google Drive links, or Vimeo).
   - **Revision Handouts**: Upload PDF reference links. The splitscreen study deck displays this document side-by-side with the lecture video. Students can print or download these handouts directly.

---

## ✏️ Part 3: Authoring Chapter Quizzes

Quizzes reinforce student retention. Keep assessments highly structural:

1. **Creating a Quiz**:
   - Inside your curriculum manager, select a lesson or chapter and click **Create Assessment Quiz**.
   - Define the **Quiz Duration** (e.g. 15 minutes) and set passing thresholds.

2. **Adding Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)**:
   - Write the question body.
   - Enter four distinct choices.
   - Designate the correct choice index (1-4).
   - **Explanation Field**: Write a detailed step-by-step conceptual answer. When students submit their attempt, the player exposes this description, helping them clear doubts immediately.

---

## 📈 Part 4: Analyzing Student Growth

1. **Progress Monitors**:
   - Review enrollment logs to see how many students are actively pursuing your class.
   - Track lesson watch averages and quiz success frequencies.

2. **Honor Boards**:
   - View top students on the **Leaderboard** to identify outstanding academic performances and award stars.
